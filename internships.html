Steps on Applying

When applying to internships, it is important to start as early as August. Make sure you have a list of all the places you want to apply to and apply to even more than that. Often times, people apply to around 200. During this time, you should be practicing for your technical interviews so make it a point to do many coding problems in your free time. Also rehearse some behavioral questions for that part of an interview. As long as you are dedicated and passionate, then your chances of getting an internship is very high.
