Want to Start Early?

Many students who are eager to pursue computer science in college will often start in college. I recommend taking courses that are related to computer science to get a head start on the field and to make sure whether this field is the right field for you. A popular course is AP Computer Science. Moreover, it is important to also immerse yourself outside of school as well.

Outside of School

There are many opportunities to satisfy your computer science interests outside of school. There are internships and programs that are geared toward beginner computer science enthusiasts. I was involved in Girls Who Code in high school which gave me a lot of exposure to the field. It is important to gain as much involvement outside of college.
